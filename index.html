FAQ Examples

Explore AI Responses: This section allows you to see how the AI Assistant responds to example customer support questions. These examples show different ways users might phrase questions and how the AI can handle them in real-time.

  • Password reset / pass change / forgot password → Reset your password via the Sign In page, choose "Existing User", click "Password Reset", enter your registered email, and follow the instructions to receive a reset link.
  • Deposit / Withdrawal / Bonus inquiry → Check your account balance, follow on-screen instructions for deposits or withdrawals. For bonuses, see Promotions page and follow eligibility and activation steps. Contact support if needed.
  • Change date of birth → Go to "My Profile" → "Settings" → "Edit Information", update your DOB, and save changes.
