FAQ Examples

Explore AI Responses: See how the AI Assistant handles example customer support questions in real-time. These examples show different ways users might phrase questions.

  • Password reset / pass change / forgot password: Reset your password via the Sign In page, choose "Existing User", click "Password Reset", enter your registered email, and follow instructions to receive a reset link.
  • Deposit / Withdrawal / Bonus inquiry: Check your account balance, follow on-screen instructions for deposits or withdrawals. For bonuses, see the Promotions page and follow eligibility and activation steps. Contact support if needed.
  • Change date of birth: Go to "My Profile" → "Settings" → "Edit Information", update your DOB, and save changes.
